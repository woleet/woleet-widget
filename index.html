Bitcoin proof verifier
Verify Data and Signatures anchored on Bitcoin

verify the existence and get the timestamp of a data anchored in the Bitcoin blockchain by any Chainpoint 1.x compatible tool.
verify the existence, the validity, the signee identity and get the timestamp of a signature anchored in the Bitcoin blockchain by any tool compatible with Woleet's signature anchoring extension to Chainpoint 1.x

If the file or signature was anchored publicly using the Woleet platform, then you don't need to provide the anchoring receipt (as it is retrieved live from the Woleet platform). Otherwise, you'll be asked to provide the Chainpoint 1.x anchoring receipt.
